\section {Derivative}
Differentiate the function f = $ {x}^{2} $\newline
It is not difficult to notice that \newline
($ x $)$ ^\prime $ = $ 1 $\newline
\newline
It is easy to lead to the form \newline
($ {x}^{2} $)$ ^\prime $ = $ 2 \cdot {x}^{(2 - 1)} \cdot 1 $ = $ 2 \cdot x $\newline
\newline
Thus we have f$^\prime$ = $ 2 \cdot x $
